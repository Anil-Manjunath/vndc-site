About The Kitchen

"The Kitchen" represents a crystallization of twenty years of research into the evolving lexicon of Modern Indian Dance. This 10-minute 23-second cinematic work interrogates the liminal spaces between tradition and contemporaneity, exploring how quotidian domestic environments become theaters of profound personal transformation. Through deliberate choreographic choices and sophisticated visual storytelling, the film excavates the inherent poetry within mundane ritualistic practices, revealing the kitchen as both sanctuary and site of existential inquiry.
