Biography

Vishwakiran Nambi is a distinguished contemporary dance artist, choreographer and dance filmmaker based in Bengaluru, India. Founder & Artistic Director of the Vishwakiran Nambi Dance Company (VNDC) and Director of Loka Studio for Movement Practices, he is a Uday Shankar Choreography Award recipient. His work spans stage, film, and education — notable works include "The Kitchen", "Pyre", "Yele Oota" and "Jaaga Illa".

Philosophy

Art breathes where breath itself begins: we believe aesthetic experience lives in everyday acts of care and craft. Our work seeks to make visible the choreographies that shape modern life, excavating contemporary experience through the body and screen.
